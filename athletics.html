    ATHLETICS

    Many Sports, One Team.

    Sports and fitness plays a central role in supporting the ethos of the school and a first class education. From practice to game day, our athletes are coached to compete with character and teamwork. In the early years, your child enjoys the sport and develops basic motor skills. During higher grades, your child will learn skill-based programmes and competitive sports. They learn to lead while respecting teammates and opponents alike and courageously push past their comfort zone to surmount the challenges - on and off the field.

    Extra-Curricular Activities

    A Proven Track Record

    Sports and fitness are vehicles for personal development. Our academic staff also encourages participation in our extensive sports programme helping the coaches produce all-star athletes. The school benefits from a team of expert professional coaches. BPS holds substantial goodwill for sports with dynamic teams, backed up by extensive playing fields and facilities.

    The major sports are cricket, basketball, football, volleyball, tennis and badminton. BPS also provides a ground for athletics and racket sports played all year round. The majority of our athletes flourish in school or house teams, while many progress to district, state and national level.

    BPS Pride — Beyond Competition

    There’s a sense of pride that comes with wearing the BPS uniform. Our student-athletes are exposed to much more than competition and tournaments. They receive endless opportunities to showcase their sports finesse through determination and discipline. They learn that determination is not towards perfection, but a commitment towards being your best version on and off the field.

    Our sports programmes regularly witness a 100% participation rate. Our participants live, breathe and practice the core values of BPS P-R-I-D-E which sets the foundation for competing at a professional level. This way, teamwork is fostered, leaders are identified and developed, our young athletes learn about themselves and others and also have lots of fun!
